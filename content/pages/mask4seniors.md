## Mask4Seniors Happening Again!

To protect the most vulnerable and the first responders in our community, we have been working hard to get more masks since Mid March. And till today (5/27), we have donated 20270 masks of various grades (N95, surgical, medical disposable, and disposable) to the community around bay area.

Beginning from 5/28, we are again accepting reservations of disposable masks. If you are of age 65+, you are eligible for one 25 masks per family (25pc in one box). These masks will be donated to you, for free.

Please fill this form and e-sign the liability release, and we will arrange mask delivery when volunteers are available (usually within 5 days):
