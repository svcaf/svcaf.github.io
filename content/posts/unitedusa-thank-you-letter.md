Since late March this year, SVCAF has collected ~$50,000 donations from the Chinese American communities to purchase and deliver masks for local charities, hospitals, police stations, small businesses, senior homes, and so on.  We just got a thank-you letter today!  For hospitals or essential businesses to accept our PPE donations, please refer to: [/pages/pperequests/](/pages/pperequests/)

### **A Warm Thank You Letter **for SVCAF’s COVID-19 Contribution

Dear SVCAF leaders,

I hope your organization is doing well amidst this special time. 

We are a small group of college students working alongside[ UnitedUSA](https://united-usa.net/) to highlight people in the AAPI community who have gone above and beyond to help others during the Covid-19 pandemic. 

On behalf of UnitedUSA, we wanted to reach out due to the incredible 15,770 masks you’ve donated to your local community and hospitals. United USA is a platform for promoting both grassroots organizations and prominent leaders in the AAPI community who are fighting tirelessly in the COVID-19 battle. We would be honored to have your organization featured on our website to pass forward the positive message along with your contribution information. 

Your accomplishments in delivering free masks for seniors and local businesses have amazed us and we would like to pay tribute to your #StrongerTogether Project by documenting stories of your donors, volunteers, and essential workers. 

Please contact us if you are willing to schedule a brief conversation to talk about SVCAF’s relief efforts.

We are grateful for your service to society and look forward to hearing from you.

Best wishes,

Yanni Zhang from the 2020 International Leadership Foundation Team
