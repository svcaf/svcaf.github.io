> *Providing education to Chinese communities on the legal and political systems in California and the nation.*

SVCAF believes an informed community is an empowered community. Since 2014, we have researched legislation, analyzed court cases, hosted seminars, and published guides — all to help Chinese Americans understand and navigate the systems that shape their lives.

---

## Current Initiatives

### 🤖 AI4Legislation (2024–Present)
A flagship program using AI tools to help community members track, analyze, and engage with legislation in real time. Includes a seminar series, a civic AI competition, and volunteer training.
