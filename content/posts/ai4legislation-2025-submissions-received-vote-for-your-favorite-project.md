We’re excited to announce that the **AI4Legislation 2025 competition** has officially closed for submissions — and we received **4 outstanding projects** from individuals and teams passionate about using AI to improve civic engagement and legislative transparency.

### **🧠 Submitted Projects**

- **Behind the Bill** – An AI-powered bill explainer that makes legislative language accessible to the public. [Video](https://youtu.be/pG4KonR68Fk) | [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/linaiyer/behind-the-bill)

- **Congressional Bill Tracker** – A machine learning dashboard that predicts the success of U.S. congressional bills using real-time data. [Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=oLsZL_xDgDU) | [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/oliversoctopus/predictive-bill-tracker-dashboard)

- **DebateSim** – A multi-agent AI debate simulator that re-creates legislative debates with real-time perspectives and an AI judge. [Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=vhyM2QNZ3VM) | [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/alexliao95311/DebateSim) | [Live Demo](https://debatesim.us/)

- **Billingo** – An interactive and fun platform for learning about bills, using AI-generated quizzes and games. [Video](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=WiKJ857K9gI) | [GitHub Repo](https://github.com/tylenode/billingo) | [Live Demo](https://billingo-demo.vercel.app)

More project details can be found here: 

* [https://github.com/svcaf/2025-AI4Legislation-Public/tree/main/submissions](https://github.com/svcaf/2025-AI4Legislation-Public/tree/main/submissions)

### **🏆 What’s Next?**

Our judging panel is currently reviewing all submissions. Final results will be announced between **August and September 2025**.

In the meantime, we’re inviting the public to help select the **People’s Choice Award** winner — a separate **$500 cash prize** will go to the project that receives the most community votes!
