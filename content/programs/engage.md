> *Encouraging active civil engagement and political participation by Chinese communities.*

Knowledge without action changes nothing. SVCAF mobilizes the community to vote, to organize, to speak up, and to fight in court when necessary. From ballot-box protection to landmark Supreme Court cases, we show up.

---

## Current Initiatives

### ⚖️ Fair Admissions Advocacy (Ongoing)
SVCAF continues to monitor and advocate for race-neutral, merit-based college admissions policies in California and across the country following the landmark *SFFA v. Harvard* victory.
