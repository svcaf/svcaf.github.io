## Empowering Chinese Americans

**Silicon Valley Chinese Association Foundation** (SVCAF) is a 501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to empowering Chinese Americans through education, civic engagement, and community action since 2015.

---

### 🎓 Education & Advocacy

Fighting for educational equity — from the Harvard admissions case to local school board advocacy. We filed amicus briefs and mobilized communities for fair admissions policies.

[Learn More →](/programs/education/)

---

### 🏛️ Civic Engagement

Encouraging Chinese Americans to actively participate in civic affairs — voter registration, policy tracking, and community organizing.
