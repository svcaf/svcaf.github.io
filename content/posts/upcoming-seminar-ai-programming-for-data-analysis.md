**Hosted by the AI4Legislation Team of SVCAF**

We are excited to invite all teams and individuals—especially those who have submitted a Letter of Intention for the [2025 AI4Legislation competition](https://github.com/svcaf/2025-AI4Legislation-Public)—to join us for an insightful seminar focused on **AI programming and data analysis**.

Whether you are a high school student, college student, or an AI-curious innovator, this session will help you understand how to integrate large language models into meaningful civic tech and policy analysis projects.

### **🗓 Event Details**

- **Topic:** *AI Programming for Data Analysis*

- **Date:** Sunday, **June 8, 2025**

- **Time:** 11:00 AM Pacific Time / 2:00 PM Eastern Time

- **Location:** Zoom (Registration Required)

- **Register Now:** [https://us06web.zoom.us/meeting/register/OIJgJMgZS_WZNadPBuimXQ](https://us06web.zoom.us/meeting/register/OIJgJMgZS_WZNadPBuimXQ)

**Registration Required**: After registering, you will receive a confirmation email with details on how to join the Zoom session.

### **🎙️ Featured Speaker:**

**Mr. Liang Guo**

AI programming and data analysis expert, with a strong background in large language model (LLM) applications, public datasets, and real-world data workflows.

### **📚 Tentative Agenda**

- What Programming Paradigm Do Large Language Models Address?

- Limitations of LLMs Compared to Traditional Programming Paradigms

- Choosing the Right LLM Model for the Right Problem

- Where to Find High-Quality Public and Government Datasets

- Using LLMs for Data Collection

- Using LLMs for Data Presentation

- Q&A Session

### **🎯 Who Should Attend?**

- Individuals or teams who submitted a **Letter of Intention** for the AI4Legislation competition

- High school and college students interested in **AI, data science, or civic tech**

- New participants considering submitting a project to the competition

- Anyone curious about **using AI to solve public policy challenges**

💡 The session will be recorded and made available to the public later.
