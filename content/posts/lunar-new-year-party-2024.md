Celebrate the Lunar New Year with the Silicon Valley Chinese Association! Celebrate our 10-year journey serving the community!

SVCA (Silicon Valley Chinese Association) Foundation wishes you a prosperous and happy Lunar New Year 2024!

Join us for an unforgettable in-person event at Chinese Performing Arts of America, San Jose on 2/10 3pm-7pm. 

Get ready to immerse yourself in the festive spirit of the Lunar New Year, filled with vibrant cultural performances, delicious food, and exciting activities. 

Meet the elected officials, community leaders and community volunteers that we helping shape our community for the better!

Whether you’re looking to reconnect with friends or make new ones, this party is the perfect opportunity to embrace the traditions and customs of this joyous occasion. Don’t miss out on this incredible celebration! 

Mark your calendars for 2/10 and get ready to welcome the Lunar New Year in style!

Date & Time: **2/10/2024 3:00-7:00PM **
Location: **6148 Bollinger Rd, San Jose, CA 95129** (Chinese Performing Arts of America)

Admission **$10** (Pay via **PayPal**: donations@svcaf.org or by **cash** at entrance, **free** for kids below 18 years old)
