> *Promoting recognition and respect of Chinese communities' contributions to society.*

The Chinese American community has contributed enormously to Silicon Valley and to the United States. SVCAF honors that legacy — celebrating achievements, standing up for individuals treated unjustly, and building a community that shows up for each other.

---

## Current Initiatives

### 🏆 Voice of Chinese Americans Award (Annual)
Our annual award honoring individuals and organizations who have made outstanding contributions to the Chinese American community and to society at large.
