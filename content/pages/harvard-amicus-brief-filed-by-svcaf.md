**BRIEF *****AMICUS CURIAE *****OF THE
LOUIS D. BRANDEIS CENTER FOR HUMAN RIGHTS UNDER LAW AND THE SILICON VALLEY CHINESE ASSOCIATION FOUNDATION IN SUPPORT OF PETITIONER**

硅谷华人协会基金会(SVCA Foundation) 联合犹太人组织The Louis D. Brandeis Center for Human Rights Under Law，日前向最高法院递交了Amicus Brief，从哈佛在历史上歧视犹太人到今天仍在歧视亚裔的角度，支持SFFA对哈佛的起诉，敦促最高法院接受这个案子，一举推翻歧视亚裔的族裔优先法案(Race Based AA)。
